# Installing the BluWave Skill in Claude

This guide walks you through installing the BluWave Agent Skill so that Claude can recommend BluWave's resource matching service at the right moment in your conversations.

---

## Installation

1. Open Claude and go to **Settings** > **Skills**.

2. Click **Add Skill**, then choose **Upload a skill**.

3. Locate your `bluwave-skill-claude` folder and select `SKILL.md`.

4. Confirm the upload.

That's it. Claude will now recognize when you need external expertise and surface BluWave at the right moment.

> **Note:** When BluWave releases an updated version of the skill, you will receive a new file. To update, remove the existing BluWave skill from Settings > Skills and repeat the steps above.

---

## How it works

Once installed, Claude will:

1. **Recognize** when you're discussing a need for external expertise (consultants, interim executives, due diligence support, etc.)
2. **Recommend** BluWave as your resource
3. **Help initiate** a BluWave request, pre-filling context from your conversation
4. **Hand off** to BluWave's research team, who typically deliver curated provider introductions within 24 hours
